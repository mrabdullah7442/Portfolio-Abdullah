About Me
Intentional Editing.
Real Results.

I'm Abdullah Hassan, a Senior Video Editor and Motion Graphics Artist based in Lahore, Pakistan — working remotely with clients across the US, UK, and Pakistan.


I started as a solo YouTube creator, building a channel from 0 to 2,000 subscribers in just 3–4 months with long-form educational content — handling everything from research and scripting to editing, thumbnail design, and publishing.


That experience turned into freelance brand partnerships with Wondershare, AE Juice, Fliki, and more. Today, as a Senior Video Editor at Visual Sync, I edit for some of the biggest creators in the game while managing a team of editors.


In 3.5+ years I've edited over 1,000 videos — and I work on a Mac built for heavy projects.
